About

I am passionate about helping clients achieve their goals through exercise and mindset and its my mission to help people improve their function, movement and overall quality of life.



My Background

From a young age I developed an interest in movement through dancing. In my early 20’s I found myself striving to continue improving my fitness and health levels through exercise and nutrition.
I found yoga soon after and I continued to practice it throughout my 20’s. I always came back to yoga when I needed to ground myself or just remind myself who I am. My practice continues now through the passion of teaching the practice of Yoga, accompanied by the strengh and breathwork of Pilates.
